About Flavorful Journey

Welcome to the heart and soul of our digital kitchen! Here at "Flavorful Journey", we're on a flavorful journey through the three islands of Mindanao, Visayas, and Luzon, exploring the rich and diverse culinary tapestry of Philippine cuisine. For every island, we've carefully selected the top four dishes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and dessert, inviting you to savor the unique flavors that define each region.

Our digital kitchen is more than just a solo performance it's a shared experience. We're building a community that celebrates the love for food and the connections it fosters. Share your own kitchen adventures, ask questions, and let's create a space where the aroma of Sinigang and the crunch of lechon skin can be experienced together, even in the virtual world. Consider this blog your virtual dining table, and join us in celebrating the vibrant and delicious journey of Philippine cuisine. Mabuhay sa inyong lahat! (Cheers to all of you!)

Who We Are

We are Allen and Gale, a dynamic duo of IT and CS students, navigating the realms of technology and culinary exploration. Our shared love for Filipino cuisine has inspired us to bridge the gap between code and flavors, creating a space where the digital and the delicious converge.

Together, we bring a blend of creativity, precision, and a deep appreciation for the cultural richness that defines Filipino cooking. As you embark on this flavorful journey with us, know that you're not just exploring recipes; you're delving into the stories, traditions, and warmth that make Filipino food a celebration. Thank you for joining us on this exciting adventure we're thrilled to have you as part of our Flavorful Journey family.

Why Kawaling CCS

We selected the name 'Kawaling CCS' with careful consideration, drawing inspiration from both our academic roots and our dedication to showcasing the authentic flavors of Filipino cuisine. As students of the Collge of Computer Studies (CCS), we wanted our blog to reflect our academic journey while seamlessly intertwining with our passion for food.

The term 'kawali' holds a special place in our hearts and kitchens. Choosing 'Kawaling CCS' not only pays homage to our department but also adds a local and distinctly Filipino touch to our culinary narrative. The 'kawali,' a traditional Filipino cooking pot, symbolizes the essence of Filipino home-cooked meals rich, flavorful, and deeply rooted in tradition. By incorporating 'kawali' into our blog name, we aim to create a digital kitchen that resonates with the warmth and authenticity of Filipino culinary heritage.

So, welcome to 'Kawaling CCS,' where the artistry of code meets the sizzle of the kawali, and where every byte is as essential as every bite. Join us on this flavorful journey as we navigate the intersection of technology and taste, proudly representing our CCS department and celebrating the diverse and delicious world of Filipino food.
